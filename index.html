About the Journal

MACOBISBANK is a scientific journal published by Universitas BPD as a platform for publishing research results, empirical studies, and theoretical reviews in Management, Economics, Business, and Banking. Published twice a year (April & October).

Focus & Scope

Economics: Public Economics, International Economics, Banking & Financial Institution, Development Economics, Monetary Economics.
Management, Business & Banking: Finance, Marketing, HRM, Strategic Management, Entrepreneurship, Banking Ethics.
Accounting: Public Sector Accounting, Taxation, Financial Accounting, Auditing, Information Systems.

Publication Ethics

MACOBISBANK applies double-blind peer review. All manuscripts are reviewed by at least two reviewers. The journal follows COPE guidelines and ensures fairness, confidentiality, and academic integrity.
